My Story

My name is Khiem, and I’m a 21-year-old student at Nha Trang University.I’m currently living in Nha Trang, and I’m passionate about software development, especially backend development. During my studies, I worked on a team project to develop a mobile application for personal finance management. The app was designed to help users track their daily, weekly, and monthly expenses so they could better manage their spending and income.

In this project, I was mainly responsible for the backend. I worked on server-side logic, database management, and API development to ensure the system operated smoothly and securely. I also collaborated closely with my teammates to improve the overall performance of the application.

What I’m most proud of is that our project received very positive feedback from our lecturers. Through this experience, I improved not only my technical abilities but also my teamwork and communication skills. Overall, I’m highly motivated to continue learning and gain more practical experience in software development..
